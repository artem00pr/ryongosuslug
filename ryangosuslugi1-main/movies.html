Movies Featuring Ryan Gosling

Drive

Release Date: September 16, 2011

Driver is a stuntman who moonlights as a getaway driver. He falls for his neighbor and gets embroiled in a heist gone wrong. This neo-noir film is known for its stylish direction and intense action sequences.

La La Land

Release Date: December 9, 2016

A jazz musician and an aspiring actress fall in love while pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles. The film is renowned for its vibrant cinematography and memorable musical numbers.

The Notebook

Release Date: June 25, 2004

A romantic drama based on Nicholas Sparks' novel about a young couple who fall deeply in love despite their different social backgrounds. The film has become a modern classic in romance cinema.

Blade Runner 2049

Release Date: October 6, 2017

A sequel to the 1982 film "Blade Runner," this sci-fi epic follows a new blade runner who uncovers a long-buried secret that could plunge what's left of society into chaos.
