Biography

Ryan Gosling is a Canadian actor and musician known for his roles in films such as "La La Land," "Drive," and "The Notebook." He began his career as a child actor on the television series "The Mickey Mouse Club" and later gained recognition for his work in film. Gosling has received numerous awards and nominations for his performances and continues to be a prominent figure in the entertainment industry.
